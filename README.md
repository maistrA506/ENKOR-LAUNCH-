## 💬 WhatsApp Pre-Order Integration

Our WhatsApp integration allows customers to easily pre-order products directly through their preferred messaging platform.

### How It Works
1. Customer clicks "Pre-Order via WhatsApp" button for their desired product
2. WhatsApp window opens with a pre-populated message
3. Customer confirms product selection and quantity
4. Message is sent to ENKOR sales team WhatsApp Business account
5. Team responds with order confirmation and payment details
6. Customer completes payment and receives delivery information

### Configuration
Update your WhatsApp business number in `js/whatsapp.js`:
```javascript
const WHATSAPP_BUSINESS_NUMBER = "+your-number-here";
const WHATSAPP_MESSAGE_TEMPLATE = "Hi, I'm interested in pre-ordering the ENKOR {PRODUCT_NAME}...";
```

### Supported Products for WhatsApp Pre-Order
- EK3161 Multimedia Speaker
- EK3160 Compact Multimedia Speaker
- EKSB61 2:1:2 CH Soundbar System
